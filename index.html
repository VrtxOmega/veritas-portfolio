From NASA-contracted thermal spray technician to VibeJam winner — in 12 weeks, on a laptop, with 1 day off.

RJ Lopez — Illinois, USA

I started coding on February 12, 2026. Before that, I was a NASA-contracted thermal spray technician — doing high-stakes quality control and precision work in cleanroom environments. I knew nothing about software.

Then I discovered AI-assisted development. I went all in. One day off in 12 weeks. All of it unpaid. All of it on a laptop that couldn't even run Android Studio — which forced me to build everything browser-native and zero-dependency.

That constraint became my competitive advantage. While other developers needed heavy IDEs, build tools, and frameworks, my code ran anywhere — even on a Chromebook. That's exactly why Breakaway won 2nd place at VibeJam 2026, the world's largest AI-coding competition with 945+ entries.

Architecture

⚡

Vanilla JavaScript

No frameworks, no build step, no bundler. Pure browser-native JS that runs instantly on any device. Refactored from 1,324-line monolith into 9 focused modules.

🔐

WebCrypto SHA-256

Browser-native cryptography for tamper-proof credential hashing. No external crypto libraries. Self-contained verification pages you can download and share offline.

🧪

Test-Driven

46 passing tests (32 scoring + 14 registry validation). GitHub Actions CI on every push. Guest mode for anonymous play with soft nudges — no forced signup.

Challenge Gallery

Credential Lifecycle

1

Challenge

User completes a cognitive skill challenge — timed, scored, validated.

2

Hash

Performance data is hashed using WebCrypto SHA-256 in the browser.

3

Sign

Credential is cryptographically signed and bound to the user's identity.

4

Store

Persisted to localStorage for offline access. Guest mode saves progress without signup.

5

Verify

Third parties can verify credential integrity by re-hashing and comparing. QR code + downloadable HTML verifier included.

6

Share

Export as portable JSON, generate QR codes, or download a self-contained verification page for instant proof.
